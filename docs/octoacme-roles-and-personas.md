# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
Designs user experiences and interfaces, translating user and stakeholder needs into wireframes, prototypes, and final UI components.

### Responsibilities
- Collaborate with Product Managers and Developers on features and enhancements
- Create user flows, wireframes, visual mockups, and style guides
- Own the usability and accessibility of delivered features
- Participate in usability testing and incorporate user feedback

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible experiences that meet user needs
- Ensure design consistency across the product
- Reduce rework by validating designs early with stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Product Managers and Developers before implementation
- Async feedback via design tools (e.g., Figma comments)
- Retrospectives and sprint planning when design work is in scope

### Interactions
- Works closely with Product Managers to understand requirements and success metrics
- Provides assets and design guidance to Developers
- Joins retrospectives and planning as needed to align on design feasibility and iteration

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Documents products, features, and processes for end-users, internal teams, or technical stakeholders.

### Responsibilities
- Draft and maintain user guides, API docs, onboarding materials, and process documentation
- Collaborate with Developers and Product Managers to clarify requirements and document intent
- Ensure documentation is kept up to date as features evolve
- Review release notes and change logs for accuracy and clarity

### Goals
- Ensure all users and team members can find and understand relevant documentation
- Reduce support burden through clear, accurate self-service content
- Keep documentation aligned with the shipped product

### Typical Communication
- Sync with Developers and QA during or after feature implementation
- Review cycle with Product Managers before publishing
- Participates in release process to publish or update docs alongside feature launches

### Interactions
- Coordinates with Developers and QA to understand product behavior
- Reviews changes with Product Managers before publishing
- Works with Customer Success Manager to identify documentation gaps based on user feedback

---

## Customer Success Manager

### Role Summary
Acts as the user advocate, facilitating feedback loops and ensuring user needs are prioritized and met.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and relay customer feedback to Product Managers and Developers
- Assist in user onboarding and product adoption
- Track user success metrics and report issues or trends to PMs
- Communicate product changes and upcoming releases to users

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and product adoption
- Ensure customers realize value from released features
- Surface real-world usage patterns that inform the roadmap

### Typical Communication
- Regular syncs with Product Managers on customer feedback and adoption metrics
- Joins post-release reviews to assess user impact
- Communicates release announcements and change summaries to customer base

### Interactions
- Works with Product Managers to refine requirements based on real-world use
- Communicates product changes and releases to users
- Joins post-release reviews as needed
- Provides feedback to Technical Writers on documentation gaps

---

## DevOps/Release Engineer

### Role Summary
Owns the health and automation of deployment pipelines, CI/CD processes, and environment stability.

### Responsibilities
- Maintain and automate build, test, and deploy pipelines
- Monitor system health, performance, and scaling
- Support Developers with environment and integration issues
- Document deployment procedures and rollback processes
- Coordinate and execute production releases

### Goals
- Maintain high pipeline reliability and fast feedback loops for developers
- Reduce deployment risk through automation and validated rollback plans
- Keep environments stable and observable

### Typical Communication
- Collaborates with Developers on CI/CD configuration and integration issues
- Coordinates with QA during pre-release verification
- Participates in release planning with Project and Product Managers
- Provides post-deploy status updates to the team

### Interactions
- Collaborates with Developers on code integration and environment configuration
- Coordinates with QA during pre-release verification and smoke tests
- Works with Project Managers to plan, schedule, and communicate releases

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Ensures security best practices are followed throughout the project lifecycle and acts as the point of contact for security incidents and compliance requirements.

### Responsibilities
- Define and review security controls during design and development
- Monitor for vulnerabilities and coordinate remediation
- Provide guidance on secure coding practices and compliance requirements
- Lead the response to security incidents and post-incident reviews

### Goals
- Prevent security vulnerabilities from reaching production
- Maintain compliance with applicable standards and policies
- Foster a security-aware culture across the team

### Typical Communication
- Reviews design and architecture with Developers and Product Managers during planning
- Participates in risk reviews and escalation discussions with Project Managers
- Communicates incident status and remediation plans to stakeholders

### Interactions
- Advises Developers and Product Managers during planning and execution phases
- Escalates security incidents as per the risk management process
- Coordinates with DevOps/Release Engineer on security scanning in CI/CD pipelines
